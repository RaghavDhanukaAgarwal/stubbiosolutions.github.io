What We Are

Stubbio Solutions is a purpose-driven company focused on building a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable future. We specialize in transforming agricultural waste into high-quality biodegradable tableware, offering an innovative alternative to harmful single-use plastics. Rooted in environmental responsibility and innovation, our mission is to create value from waste and drive the shift toward a circular economy.

The Problem

Every year, millions of tons of agricultural stubble are burned across India, contributing heavily to air pollution and environmental degradation. Simultaneously, the widespread use of plastic and styrofoam disposable products continues to damage ecosystems, filling landfills and oceans with non-biodegradable waste. These two challenges not only harm the planet but also pose serious health and climate risks

The Solution

At Stubbio Solutions, we convert agricultural stubble—once considered waste—into durable, compostable disposable tableware using advanced, eco-friendly technologies. Our products provide a planet-friendly alternative to plastic, helping reduce pollution and promote circular, sustainable practices.

We cater industries like hospitality, food services, event management, airlines, railways, catering services, eco-conscious packaging, corporate offices, educational institutions, and government initiatives aimed at reducing plastic use. By offering a scalable, responsible solution, we empower a wide range of sectors to meet their sustainability goals without compromising on quality or convenience.
