From Fields to Future: Harvesting Changes

We convert stubble into eco-friendly disposable tableware.

What We Are

We are a Eco-conscious startup who wants to make a change. Our startup is focused on providing an innovative and environmentally sustainable solution to the problem of stubble burning and single-use plastic waste. Every year, millions of tons of agricultural stubble are burned across India, causing severe air pollution and environmental degradation. At the same time, the growing demand for disposable tableware contributes significantly to plastic waste, further harming the environment. Our solution lies in repurposing agricultural stubble, a byproduct of farming, and converting it into biodegradable, compostable disposable tableware.
